The meeting with Newell's Old Boys will please not only Messi, but also Gerardo Martino. The Inter coach has almost 500 appearances for the club

Inter announced that the meeting with Newell's Old Boys will take place on February 15, 2024 - approximately a couple of weeks before the start of the new MLS competition.

Newell's Old Boys are based in Rosario, the hometown of Leo Messi and Inter Miami head coach Gerardo Martino. The club is no stranger to both: 13-year-old Messi moved from there to the Barcelona academy, and Martino played almost 500 matches and coached him from 2012 to 2013.

In the summer, Messi already traveled to his homeland for the farewell match of Maxi Rodriguez - he both started and ended his career at Newell's Old Boys. Leo played for Argentina and celebrated his 36th birthday with a half-time hat-trick.
